The beginning of a great adventure

Beginnings of the Story

First...

Everything falls apart

Leín’s childhood is shattered by the sudden loss of his biological parents, throwing him into a cold and unknown reality. In the face of grief and humiliation, he is forced to survive in a world that seems to push him further into isolation.

“Life broke into pieces, and I fell among them.”
Loading...
Then...

The false home

Forced to adapt to a toxic adoptive family, Leín endures constant emotional abuse and loneliness. Building a shell to protect himself, he slowly becomes a stranger in his own life, never truly belonging.

“I was a stranger in my own home.”
Loading...
Next...

Inner escape

Finding refuge in creativity, Leín builds alternative worlds in his mind—his only sanctuary from pain. There, imagination becomes survival and keeps hope alive while the outside world remains hostile.

“Inside me, I built a world where I could finally breathe.”
Loading...
Finally...

The Adventure Begins

Tired of hiding and pretending, Leín finally escapes his oppressive home and throws himself into the world, determined to discover his true essence. Amid uncertainty and fear, he takes his first steps into freedom, willing to live by his own rules.

“I risked everything to discover who I really am.”

The Sacred Elements

Deep Self-Reflection

Looking inward is to descend into your own roots, where silence reveals what was never spoken.
Truth peeks out when you don’t look away.

Omniscient Technology

In modern chaos, learning to listen to digital whispers can become the soul’s subtle compass.
Between lines of text, sometimes signals bloom.

Emotional Maturity

Allowing yourself to feel is to cross the threshold, where every tear and every laugh opens new doors in your own temple.
Emotions are the secret language of change.

Search for Purpose

The knowledge of what you must do reveals itself in the most unexpected folds of the journey.
Purpose is the greatest compass when you are lost.

World Exploration

Travels and geographic spaces can open doors to places never imagined.
There’s no difference between inner and outer exploration.

Soul Mentors

There are ancient voices and friendly glances at the crossroads, able to illuminate what seems irresolvable.
Other people’s wisdom can be the beacon you long for.
